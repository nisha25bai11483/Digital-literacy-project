Social Media Do’s:
1. Use respectful and polite language  
2. Keep your profile professional  
3. Think before posting  
4. Protect your personal information  
5. Follow positive and educational content  

Social Media Don’ts:
1. Don’t share passwords  
2. Don’t post inappropriate content  
3. Don’t spread fake news  
4. Don’t engage in online arguments  
5. Don’t overshare personal details
