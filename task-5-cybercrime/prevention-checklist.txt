Stay Safe Online Checklist:

1. Never share your passwords with anyone  
2. Always verify the sender before clicking on links  
3. Avoid clicking on suspicious emails or messages  
4. Use strong and unique passwords  
5. Enable two-factor authentication where possible  
6. Do not share personal information on unknown websites  
7. Regularly update your apps and software  
8. Be careful while using public Wi-Fi  

UPI Safety Tips:
9. Never share your OTP or UPI PIN  
10. Always double-check payment requests before approving  

Report cybercrime at: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
Helpline Number: 1930
