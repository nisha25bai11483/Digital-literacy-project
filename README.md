Project Overview
This project was created as part of the Digital Literacy course to develop essential skills required in today’s digital world. The aim of this project is to understand how to use technology effectively, communicate professionally, and stay safe online.

Through this project, I explored various digital platforms, created informative content, and learned how to maintain a professional online presence. Each task helped me gain practical knowledge that will be useful in my academic and professional journey.

 Tasks Completed

Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic
In this task, I designed an infographic using Canva to explain the concept of digital literacy. The infographic includes important topics such as safe internet practices, useful digital tools, and professional online behavior.


Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio
I created and updated my profiles on GitHub and LinkedIn. These platforms help in building a professional identity and showcasing skills, projects, and achievements.

Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms
I explored HackerRank and completed a beginner-level coding challenge. I also created a Google Form quiz on digital literacy to understand how online collaboration tools work.

Task 4 – Email Etiquette & Social Media Guide
I drafted two professional emails, one for requesting an assignment extension and another for applying for an internship. I also created a checklist of do’s and don’ts for responsible social media usage.

Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness
In this task, I studied phishing as a type of cybercrime and created a case study explaining how it works. I also prepared a checklist to help students stay safe online and avoid such threats.

 Key Learnings
- Importance of digital literacy in daily life  
- How to use online tools effectively  
- Basics of professional communication  
- Awareness about cyber safety and online threats
